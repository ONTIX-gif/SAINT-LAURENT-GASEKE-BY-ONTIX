Frequently Asked Questions

When does admissions open?

Admissions typically open in June for the upcoming academic year. Early application is encouraged as seats are limited.

What are the accommodation options?

We offer both on-campus dormitory facilities and support for off-campus housing. Accommodation is optional but recommended.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes, merit-based scholarships are available for outstanding students. Contact admissions for specific criteria and application process.
